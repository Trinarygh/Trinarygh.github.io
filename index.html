Habdbill Features

Features for users

The moment you become a user on handbill, you have the freedom to select a category of ads that interests you. On Handbill you earn rewards in the form of money by engaging with your favourite brands. On handbill you can also become an agent to warehousemen(advertisers) by selling products that they put on offer. The warehousemen pay a commission to you for selling their products.

Features for advertisers

The moment you become an advertiser on handbill, you have the liberty to publish ads of your product and services. On handbill you pay users for engaging with your ads. Also if you want to go beyond advertising your products, Handbill offers you the opportunity to become a warehouseman. User turned agents will help you sell your products that you store in your warehouse.
